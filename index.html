Everything you need

Simple tools to manage your monthly budget with confidence

Monthly Budgets

Create a budget for each month with planned income and expenses. Color-code months with beautiful themes.

Income Tracking

Set your planned income and record actual earnings. See the variance at a glance to stay informed.

Expense Categories

Organize spending with built-in categories like food, housing, and transport — or create your own custom ones.

Visual Analytics

Beautiful charts show monthly trends, category breakdowns, and yearly summaries so you see the full picture.

Templates

Save recurring expenses as templates. Apply them when creating new months to save time every cycle.

iCloud Sync

Your data syncs seamlessly across all your Apple devices via iCloud. Start on iPhone, continue on iPad.
